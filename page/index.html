Straw Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates are a pirate crew led by Monkey D. Luffy. The crew was formed in the East Blue and has grown to become one of the most powerful and influential pirate crews in the world. Their goal is to find the legendary treasure known as the One Piece and make Luffy the King of the Pirates.

The crew consists of powerful fighters, navigators, doctors, shipwrights, musicians, and more. Together, they have defeated powerful enemies and formed strong alliances across the seas.
